# 🛍️ SHEIN Data Extraction Solution

We provide high-fidelity, structured, and scalable data pipelines to help e-commerce brands, market researchers, and dropshippers track retail trends, monitor pricing updates, and optimize inventory management in real-time.

## 🚀 Key Capabilities
* **Full Product Catalog Scraping:** Extract comprehensive product details including Titles, Descriptions, SKUs, Categories, and Images.
* **Real-Time Price & Discount Tracking:** Monitor original prices, sale prices, active coupons, and percentage discounts across different regions.
* **Variant & Inventory Mapping:** Track stock availability, sizes, colors, and specific variant URLs.
* **Customer Sentiment Analysis:** Scrape product ratings, reviews, and user-uploaded media to gauge market demand and product quality.

## 📊 Available Data Fields
We deliver clean, analysis-ready data in your preferred format (**CSV, Excel, or Database integration**). The fields we regularly extract include:
* **Product Information:** Product ID, Name, Main Category, Sub-category, and Description.
* **Pricing & Currency:** Original Price, Sale Price, Active Discounts, and Currency used.
* **Stock & Options:** Sizes Available, Colors, Stock Status (In Stock / Out of Stock), and Variant URLs.
* **Social Proof:** Product Ratings, Total Review Count, and Customer Review Text.
